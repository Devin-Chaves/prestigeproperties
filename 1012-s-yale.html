Neighborhood Details

Hilltop

Located on the southeast side of the City of Wichita, Hilltop is densely concentrated neighborhood with a unique history. The area's boundaries are Oliver to the East, Broadview to the West, Harry to the South, and Lincoln to the North.

The Hilltop Neighborhood Revitalization Program achieved 5 goals in the last 10 years:
  • Preserved Community Policing
  • Preserved Community Garden, Park, Historic Resources, and Church
  • Added Programs for Youths
  • Added More Park and Street Lights
  • Removed Abandoned Homes
